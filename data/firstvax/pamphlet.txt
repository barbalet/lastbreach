WHAT WE DEMAND (NOT “DEBATE”)
-----------------------------
We don’t demand perfect decisions made in panic.
We demand honest accounting after the panic.

We demand standards that can’t be waived by vibes.

TRUTH STANDARDS
- Public definitions for the numbers, and a public record when they change.
- Independent audits of data, procurement, and enforcement.
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure with consequences.
- Protection for whistleblowers, clinicians, and scientists who disagree.

POWER STANDARDS
- Sunset clauses that actually end.
- Clear thresholds for restrictions and clear criteria for ending them.
- No retroactive immunity after mass harm—ever.
- Compensation paths that don’t require a decade and a lawyer.

LIFE STANDARDS
If you claim the crisis “ended” and life didn’t improve, you harvested it.
Prove improvement:
- staffed healthcare
- paid sick leave
- resilient eldercare
- long-term disability support
- clean indoor air as infrastructure, not luxury
